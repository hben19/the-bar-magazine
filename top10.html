Seymour's Parlour - W1H 7JB
Customer Service:
Atmosphere:
Drink quality:
The lounge is dark and sumptuous, feeling more like the private residence of a notorious ancestor. Amazing drinks, amazing service and the best possible atmosphere.
2
Buddha Bar - E63 DN
Customer Service:
Atmosphere:
Drink quality:
Buuddha bar isn't just one of the best secret bars in London, but one of the best full stop. Offering a wide range of cocktails, local craft beers, delicious street food and a Sunday roast to die for, there's so much to enjoy here already.
3
Dinosaurs Bar - E14 6NH
Customer Service:
Atmosphere:
Drink quality:
Dinosaurs bar brings a simple, unfussy approach to drinking in Soho. Located in one of the most sought after districts in London, the ground floor wine bar brings high quality bottles of wine to the fore. Upstairs is where the focus shifts to mixology, with classic and original tipples served up from 5pm.
4
Jungle Bar - E1 0NJ
Customer Service:
Atmosphere:
Drink quality:
Nestled in the heart of Shoreditch, Jungle bar is a quirky, cocktail favourite, where talented bartenders mix up some of the most imaginative cocktails in London.

Atmosphere:
Drink quality:
